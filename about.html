✦ Our Story ✦

Born from a love of nature

Leish Essentials started as a passion project — a desire to create clean, natural products that actually work and feel luxurious. Every candle poured, every soap crafted and every gloss made is a little piece of us, made just for you.

Why Leish Essentials?

We believe that what you put on your body matters just as much as what you put in it. That's why every single product in our range is made with natural ingredients — no hidden nasties, no harsh chemicals. Just pure, honest goodness crafted with intention. 🌿

From our hand-poured candles to our nourishing soaps and glossy lip treats — every product is a labour of love. We're not just selling products, we're sharing a lifestyle rooted in self-care, nature and intentional living.
