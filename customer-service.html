🌿 We keep it simple! No account needed, no complicated checkout. Just browse, add to cart, fill in a form and we handle the rest.

Step 1 — Browse & Add to Cart

Head to our Shop page and click "Add to Cart 🛒" on any product you love. You can add as many items as you like — your cart is saved automatically.

Step 2 — Review Your Cart

Click the 🛒 icon at the top of the page to view your cart. Here you can adjust quantities, remove items and choose your shipping option.

Step 3 — Choose Your Shipping

Select whether you're based within Gauteng or outside Gauteng, then choose between Standard or Premium/Express shipping. Your delivery fee will be calculated automatically.

Step 4 — Place Your Order

Click "Place Order" and you'll be required to fill in your name, email address,phone number, delivery address and any special notes.

Step 5 — Receive Your Confirmation

After submitting your form, we'll email you:

  • Your full order summary
  • Our banking details for payment
  • An estimated delivery timeframe

Step 6 — Make Your Payment

Pay via EFT using the banking details we send you. Please use your name as the payment reference so we can match your payment to your order.

Step 7 — We Get to Work! 🌿

Once payment is confirmed, we prepare your order with love and dispatch it within 5-7 business days. You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order is on its way.

📍 We're based in Pretoria, Gauteng and we deliver across all of South Africa. Gauteng customers enjoy lower shipping rates and faster delivery times!

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 5-7 business days after payment confirmation. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays will be processed on the next business day.

How Shipping Works

Your shipping fee is calculated automatically in your cart based on your location and chosen shipping type. Once you place your order via our form, delivery is arranged after payment has been received and confirmed.

Order Tracking

Once your order has been dispatched, we'll send you a tracking number via email so you can follow your parcel's journey all the way to your door.

Please Note

  • We deliver to physical addresses only — no PO Boxes
  • Leish Essentials is not responsible for delays caused by the courier
  • Always double-check your delivery address when placing your order
  • Delivery times are estimates and may vary during peak periods
💛 We take great pride in every product we make. If something isn't right, we will always do our best to make it right for you.

What Can Be Returned

We accept returns under the following circumstances:

  • Items that arrive damaged or defective
  • Incorrect items sent (wrong product or wrong variant)
  • Items that are significantly different from what was described on our website

What Cannot Be Returned

For hygiene and safety reasons, we cannot accept returns on:

  • Products that have been used or opened
  • Items purchased during a sale or special
  • Items returned after 7 days of delivery
  • Items without original packaging

How to Start a Return

Contact us via our Contact Page within 7 days of receiving your order. Please include:

  • Your full name and email address
  • Your order details
  • The reason for the return
  • A clear photo if the item arrived damaged

Return Shipping

Return shipping costs are covered by the customer unless the item was damaged or incorrect — in which case we cover the cost.

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, your refund or replacement will be processed within 5–7 business days. Refunds are issued via EFT to your bank account. Original shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

🔒 Your privacy matters to us. We are transparent about what we collect, why we collect it and how we use it. We will never sell your information.

Information We Collect

When you place an order or contact us, we collect:

  • Your full name
  • Email address
  • Physical delivery address
  • Order details and any notes you provide

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfil your order
  • To communicate with you about your order status
  • To send you your tracking information
  • To respond to any enquiries you send us

What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not sell your personal information to any third parties
  • We do not store your payment details — payments are handled via EFT
  • We do not send marketing emails without your consent
  • We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics on our website

Data Security

Your information is handled with the utmost care. We use secure, trusted platforms (Formspree and Google Forms) to collect your data. We do not store any sensitive payment information on our end.

Cookies & Local Storage

Our website uses localStorage only to save your cart items between visits so you don't lose your selections. No tracking cookies or third-party analytics tools are used on this website.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us via our Contact Page.
